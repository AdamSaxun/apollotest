The Heritage

Our Story

1960s

The Godfather's Distillery

In the rural heartlands of Slovakia, a family tradition begins. The grandfather's copper pot still transforms humble sugar beets into something extraordinary — a spirit with the unmistakable taste of the land itself.

1990s

Recipes Passed Down

Through decades of refinement, the family recipes are carefully preserved and passed to the next generation — each iteration more precise, yet always faithful to the original method and flavor.

2018

A Bartender's Vision

Maroš Potůček, awarded Best Bartender in Slovakia, discovers the family distillate. He sees not just a spirit, but a story — one that deserves to be told on the world stage.

Today

Apollo Is Born

Heritage meets innovation. Apollo Spirit launches as a premium European beet spirit — handcrafted, locally sourced, and designed for the modern cocktail renaissance.

Beet to Bottle

The Process

  1. 01

    Harvest

    Sugar beets are hand-selected from regional farms at peak ripeness, when natural sugar content is highest.

  2. 02

    Manual Prep

    Each beet is washed, peeled, and sliced by hand — a deliberate process that honours the raw material.

  3. 03

    Enzymatic Fermentation

    Natural enzymes break down starches into fermentable sugars over days — patience is the secret ingredient.

  4. 04

    Copper Distillation

    Double-distilled in a hand-forged copper pot still, the spirit emerges pure, smooth, and full of terroir character.
